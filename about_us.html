About Our Charity

Hope House International is a childrenâ€™s home based in Kitale, Kenya founded in 2009 by Bishop Rev Peter Yuol Gur from Tonj, South Sudan. The facility currently houses 38 children and youth aged between the ages of 4years to 18 years.This comprises of 16 girls and 22 boys. What began as support for two young boys namely Gabriel T.
