About Hope House

Hope House International is a registered community based organisation and charity in south Sudan since 23rd September, 2018 by the Relief and Rehabilitation Commission (RRC) and in Trans Nzoia County,Kenya ,on 3rd August,2022. Our first centre is in Kitale with 43 orphans,and vulnerable youth we have been supporting since 2014 for education,shelter ,food ,medical care and clothes. We have been appealing to our sponsors/donors/volunteers who have been working with us over time and majority have bought the ideas of initiating a return to our country from the asylum seeking in Kenya. Our Wau centre project is on a roll out .Kelly Academy of Hope building project is ongoing sponsored by a family from the United Kingdom to construct 6 classrooms. The same family helped in purchasing the land in 2020. It's a sizable piece of land that can take both primary and secondary school(future proposal)together as well as our centre or director's family.

About Our Charity

As an institution, we would like to keep our support for education in fees provisions but not including taking a whole responsibility of children like in our Kitale ,Kenya centre. Having a community school where we can allow our community around us bring their children and our children we have taken the responsibility on to learn at ease but have them to return to their relatives' home after boarding school for long holiday. Now that we are back into the country as a legal identity, we aims to expands our areas of outreach programs around continuing to support orphans and vulnerable children for education services, shelter ,medical and clothes. We will further wish to do much work revolving children rights and advocacy in partnership with state and national ministries of social welfares and justice(coiling friendship where possible), girlchild rights and force marriage legal protection,youth and women empowerment, civil education and human rights,Ethical and Christian trainings for pastors as partners(gospels outreach programs),sports and cultural to mend peace and development.
